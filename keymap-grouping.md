`Ctrl + Meta`
- __Rectangle__

`Ctrl + Shift`
- *all apps* (mainly vim)

`Meta + Shift`
- *all apps* (mainly vim)

`Cmd + Ctrl`
- MacOS: App shortcuts
- __item__: 
    - `Arrows` for resize
- __Aerospace__:
    - `hjkl` navigate

`Cmd + Meta`
- __item__:
    - `Arrows` for pane change

`Cmd + Shift`
- MacOS: 3-5 for screenshots
- __item__:
    - `Arrows` and `[]` for tab change
    - `D` for pane split
    - `Enter` for maximize
